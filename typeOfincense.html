Types of Incense

Discover the diverse world of incense, each type carrying its own unique tradition and purpose.

Traditional Incense

Traditional incense represents centuries-old craftsmanship, typically made from natural aromatic materials bound together. These sticks or coils are widely used in religious ceremonies, meditation, and daily aromatherapy.

  • Natural ingredients
  • Long-lasting fragrance
  • Various aromatic profiles

Buddhist Incense

Buddhist incense, known for its pure and subtle fragrances, plays a vital role in temple ceremonies and meditation practices. These specially crafted sticks often contain precious woods and resins chosen for their spiritual properties.

  • Pure botanical ingredients
  • Subtle, refined scents
  • Traditional formulations

Cone Incense

Cone incense offers a unique burning experience with its compact shape and concentrated fragrance. Perfect for shorter sessions, these cones provide a strong initial burst of scent that gradually mellows into a sustained aromatic presence.

  • Concentrated fragrance
  • Ideal for small spaces
  • Modern interpretation
